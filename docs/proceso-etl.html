Datasets Principales:

  • IA_impact_on_jobs.csv: Análisis del impacto en roles laborales

  • corporate-investment-in-artificial-intelligence-by-type.csv: Inversión global
  • newly-funded-artificial-intelligence-companies.csv: Startups de IA
